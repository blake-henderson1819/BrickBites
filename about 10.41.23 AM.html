About the Creator

Blake Henderson

Engineering student, food innovator, and the mind behind Brick Bites.

Hi, I’m Blake — a bioengineering student at Oregon State University with a passion for whole, sustainable food. I created Brick Bites to give busy students real nutrition without the price tag or the prep time. When I’m not in the lab or the kitchen, you might find me trail running, tinkering with DIY projects, or growing fungi.

This idea started as a way to stop having to meal prep so much, but quickly became something I wanted to share with others. I'm obsessed with this idea and I am going to drive myself crazy pursuing it

I appreciate you stopping by — and for supporting the project.
